ABOUT US

Quantum Service and Logistics (QSL) is an Australian owned private company and we provide after sales services to the electronics companies and their customers, within the Asia Pacific region.

Our services are customised and fully integrated with a single point of contact for a range of services to manage the after sales obligations of our partners.

The company was established in 2004 and has since, experienced significant growth through expansion in service offerings, superior performances and establishment of a regional footprint within Asia Pacific countries.

We are flexible, approachable and will work with our partners to develop the right solution for their business needs.

REGIONAL FOOTPRINT

QSL has established formal strategic alliance partnerships with like minded and cultured companies, throughout the Asia Pacific countries. The purpose of this alliance is to provide a range of services to our partners across the region, on a single point of contact and ownership.

    Additional benefits of this alliance are:
  • Single contact for all account management across the region
  • Single standard of service and quality
  • Knowledge share among partners in every country
  • Efficiency in spare parts procurement and inventory management
  • Structured but flexible and approachable for customisedservices in each country
